Welcome to Viña del Mar

Viña del Mar is a coastal tourist city northwest of Santiago de Chile. It is known for its gardens, beaches, and tall buildings. In the Quinta Vergara Park you will find the Quinta Vergara Amphitheater, the Vergara Palace from the beginning of the 20th century and the Artequin Museum, which exhibits copies of important works of art. The Francisco Fonck Museum of Archeology and History has stone Moai sculptures from Easter Island and reduced heads.

Today it is famous for its international tourist attraction, this coastal city was located in what were once two colonial estates. It began to emerge as a city at the end of the 19th century, becoming a spa for high society.

The city of tourism

The Vergara Pier

The Vergara Pier is an old structure for industrial use and a tourist landmark in the city of Viña del Mar, Valparaíso Region, Chile.
