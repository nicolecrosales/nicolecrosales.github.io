Audience

The audience is adults citizen who live in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven and tourists who planned to visit them.



They are between 18 and 60 years old, who have acces to internet and knowing manipulate a mobile and desktop.



They have an annual income from $10.000 and they want to be prepared to pass a good day and planning activities outside.
